»» Congratulations, Dale! ««
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A Shining Example of Dedication

Dale poses for his official portrait
in the MCHS library (Photo: J. Henderson)

It is with great pride and enthusiasm that Millbrook Central High School announces our 2025 Janitor of the Year -- our very own Dale Ringus! For the past eleven years, Dale has been the quiet backbone of our school, arriving before the first bell and staying long after the final whistle on Friday nights.

Whether it's a spilled carton of chocolate milk in the cafeteria, an unfortunate incident with the 7th grade science fair volcano, or the annual post-homecoming gymnasium cleanup, Dale has handled every challenge with the same calm demeanor and steady hand that has made him a legend in the MCHS hallways.

"Dale is more than a custodian. He's a fixture. I once saw him mop an entire hallway in the time it takes me to microwave a Lean Cuisine. The man is a professional."
— Principal Margaret Whitaker

Selected from a pool of three qualified candidates by the Millbrook County Facilities Excellence Committee, Dale was recognized for his outstanding floor care, his uncanny ability to locate lost retainers, and his signature lemon-scented hallway fragrance that students and faculty have come to associate with the feeling of a freshly cleaned school.

★ Fun Facts About Dale ★

  • Years at MCHS: 11
  • Favorite Mop: The Rubbermaid Commercial Grade #24 (pictured)
  • Secret Talent: Can identify any cafeteria spill by smell alone
  • Hobbies: Fishing, restoring vintage Pontiacs, his band "Dale & The Deep Cleaners"
  • Favorite Hallway: The east wing by the trophy case ("Best acoustics")
  • Known For: Always having exactly the right tool on his belt

Dale will be honored at the annual Staff Appreciation Assembly on Friday, November 21st in the main gymnasium. All students, parents, and faculty are encouraged to attend. Light refreshments will be provided by the PTA, and Mrs. Donnelly's Home Ec class will be baking the cake. Please no silly string this year -- someone has to clean it up.

📰 School News & Announcements

Marching Band Takes 2nd at Regional Competition!

Posted: October 9, 2025

The MCHS Marching Beavers brought home a silver medal this weekend in Danville. Special recognition to senior drum major Amanda Reyes for her outstanding leadership. Bus pickup for the victory breakfast is at 7:15 AM sharp from the south lot.

Reminder: Fall Picture Retakes

Posted: October 7, 2025

Students who missed picture day or weren't satisfied with their photos can have retakes done on October 14th during 2nd and 3rd period. Order forms are available in the main office. Checks payable to Lifetouch, please.

Gymnasium Closed Wed PM for Waxing

Posted: October 6, 2025

The main gym will be closed Wednesday after 4 PM so that Dale and his crew can apply the annual floor wax. No PE classes or after-school athletics in that space. Basketball practice has been moved to the auxiliary gym. Thank you for your patience!
